On this website I try to focus on more advanced topics.

Most of real-life problems cannot be easily solved using mathematics and we need to resort to numerical integration. For example you might want to simulate physics of rigid and soft bodies, flow of liquid and gas or motion of celestial bodies. Once you write the appropriate algorithm, you probably want to see the results in some meaningful way, right? You will likely need to know a bit of computer graphics to produce 3D scenes. You might also want to use the power of modern GPUs to accelerate the computations.
